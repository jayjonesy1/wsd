Doctor Who

This is a fansite for the British T.V show Doctor Who. Originally running between 1963 and 1989 before taking a hiatus for 7 years. However it only returned for a one off special in 1996 before disappearing again until it was reborn in 2005. This site is designed to provide you a small bit of info about the hit show from classic Who to the new modern rebirth.

About

Starting in 1963 on BBC in the United Kingdom, featuring a man known only as The Doctor. He is a timelord from the planet Gallifrey. The Doctor travels through time and space in his machine the TARDIS, Time and Relative Dimension in Space. The Doctor travels through periods of time on earth and on other planets, meeting humans and aliens. Some he even keeps as a companion to travel with him. The Doctor is essentially just a man travelling to see the universe and he ends up helping people and saving the world from bad situations. The Doctor has lived a long time and has been killed by different means quite a few times. When The Doctor dies, his body regenerates into a new form. He keeps his memories and knowledge and experiences but gains a new personality and a new look. So far there has been 11 different Doctors with a 12th just recently announced.

BBC

BBC stands for British Broadcasting Corporation, and is a television network in the United Kingdom.

The Doctors

  • First Doctor - William Hartnell Nov 1963/Oct 1966
  • Second Doctor - Patrick Troughton Oct 1966/June 1969
  • Third Doctor - Jon Pertwee Jan 1970/June 1974
  • Fourth Doctor - Tom Baker June 1974/March 1981
  • Fifth Doctor - Peter Davison March 1981/March 1984
  • Sixth Doctor - Colin Baker March 1984/December 1986
  • Seventh Doctor - Sylvester McCoy September 1987/December 1989
  • Eighth Doctor - Paul McGann May 1996
  • Ninth Doctor - Christopher Eccleston March 2005/June 2005
  • Tenth Doctor - David Tennant June 2005/January 2010
  • Eleventh Doctor - Matt Smith January 2010/December 2013
  • Twelfth Doctor - Peter Capaldi December 2013
